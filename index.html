Have you ever been downtown and haven’t found a table for dinner? Find a table is an app which will enable you to find free tables around town. Just type wherever you are and our system will offer a wide range of restaurants and their availability at that moment. Find a table is a handy and reliable app. It’s quite simple; find a table, book it and go!!

FAQ

What is Find a Table?
Find a table is an app which will let you see availability of restaurants in the area where you are.
How does it work?
Download our app on Google Play or Apple store. When you start the app, you will see the restaurants nearby. You can check their availability, as well as information about the restaurant, opinions and a free option for booking a table.
Do I have to pay?
No. Find a table is a free app.
